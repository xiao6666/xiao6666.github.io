Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our price matching guarantee

What information do I need to provide for a price match?

To process your price match request, we require a detailed quote from the competitor that includes the fabric specifications, dimensions, hardware details, and styling elements. For the most accurate comparison, please provide fabric swatches if available.

How long does the price matching process take?

Our design consultants typically complete the evaluation and present a custom proposal within 48 hours of receiving your competitor's quote. For complex designs or specialty fabrics, this process may take up to 72 hours.

What if you don't have an exact match for the competitor's fabric?

In cases where an exact match isn't available, we'll offer alternatives of equivalent or superior quality that maintain the aesthetic and functional properties you desire. Our textile specialists carefully select options that enhance your design vision while honoring our price match guarantee.

Does the price match apply to all competitors?

Our price matching applies to quotes from legitimate custom window treatment providers with verifiable business operations. We reserve the right to exclude quotes from liquidation sales, closeouts, or businesses that don't provide custom, made-to-measure window treatments.

Can I combine the price match with other promotions?

Our 20% price match discount represents our best possible value and cannot be combined with other promotional offers, seasonal discounts, or loyalty rewards. However, our complimentary design consultation and installation services remain included with your price match order.
